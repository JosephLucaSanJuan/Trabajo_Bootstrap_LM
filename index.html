Introduction

The Morrison Formation is one of the largest and and richest sources of dinosaur fossils in North America.

It spreads across the states of South Dakota, Wyoming, Utah, Oklahoma, Texas, New Mexico Colorado, etc. and dates back to the Late Jurassic Period, from 156 till 146 million years ago.

Some of the most famous dinosaurs of all time have been found here, incluiding Stegosaurus, Allosaurus and Diplodocus. Other fossil sites with similar fauna include the Lourinha Formation in Portugal and the Tendaguru formation in Tanzania

Garden Park Colorado

One of the most important locations regarding the study of Late Jurassic dinosaurs.

The park is located near the town of Colorado City, and scientist have been digging there since 1877.

Among the dinosaurs found there include the first complete Allosaurus skeleton as well as the fist known remains of Ceratosaurus, Camarasaurus and Diplodocus

Dry Mesa Quarry

The site was first described as a fossil site when the bone of the theropod Torvosaurus tanneri were found, thus leading to more quarying operations taking place from 1972 till 1976, during which a documentary called The Great Dinosaur Discovery was filmed showcasing the discoveries made there.

Around 4000 bone have been collected, among them 23 genera o dinosaurs, incluiding Ceratosaurus, Dryosaurus, Allosaurus; and, the mosnt celebrated find, Supersaurus, one of the longest dinosaur ever found

Cleveland Lloyd Dinosaur Quarry

Located at Jurassic National Monumetn in Utah, it is one of the densest concentrations of Jurassic dinosaur bones ever found, with over 74 individuals dinos found.

Most of the bones belong to carnivores, specifically 44 of the dinosaurs found being Allosaurus, which has lead scientist to devise many hypothesis on how they died.

So far the most common theories are that the dinosaurs became stuck in mud while trying to drink from a lake, which attracted predators that also became trapped; or several dinosaurs being brought to a lakebed due to a massive drought, and with all the carnivores in one spot, the hervibores began to leave, which led to the Allosaurus turning on and canibalising each other.
